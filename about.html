NEW LUCKY ELECTRICALS is a company that deals in Electrical products and was incorporated in Ghana in 1996 Currently, the company has fourteen branches scattered in Accra with its head office situated at West Legon near the ABSA Bank. By dint of hard work and good reputation, the company, won the trust and admiration of many local and foreign suppliers of electrical products. It serves as an agent for some world renowned brands of electrical products such as Borsan, Liper, Havells, Polycab, C&S Electric, Orient Electric, Oktay Lighting, Mutlusan Electric, Horoz Electric, Nexans Kabelmental, Sneha, MHL Lighting, Nak Kablo, among others. The company also has its own products with its brand name under OEM (original equipment manufacturer) classification. These include; Everplast PVC pipes, NLE origin cables and lightening products, Klass and Classic switches
